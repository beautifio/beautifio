# BEAUTIFIO RESET REPORT

**Date:** 2026-06-11
**Purpose:** Understand what Beautifio IS today, separate from what it WAS or SHOULD BE.

---

## PART 1 — WHAT IS BEAUTIFIO TODAY?

**"Beautifio is a dream-journey tracker where users pick from 10 hardcoded dream templates, complete daily activities across 6 life dimensions, track big/small wins, and write daily reflections — with disconnected secondary features for stories, mentors, circles, and a voucher marketplace."**

---

## PART 2 — PRIMARY USER JOURNEY

```
Register ──► Welcome ──► /journey
                              │
                              ├── Has active journey? ──► /journey/[id]
                              │                              │
                              │                              ├── Daily activities (checklist)
                              │                              ├── Big wins + small wins
                              │                              ├── Daily reflection
                              │                              ├── Timeline / story
                              │                              └── Alternative futures view
                              │
                              └── No active journey? ──► Template list (10 dreams)
                                                              │
                                                              └── Tap "Pilih Mimpi Ini"
                                                                      │
                                                                      └── createJourney()
                                                                              │
                                                                              ├── INSERT dream_journey
                                                                              ├── BATCH INSERT big_wins (age-filtered)
                                                                              ├── BATCH INSERT small_wins
                                                                              └── INSERT 6 daily_activities
                                                                                      │
                                                                                      └── redirect /journey/[id]
```

**What's NOT in this journey:**
- Dream discovery (no quiz, no personality match, no guided exploration — just a list)
- Onboarding (7-step form exists but creates no journey — dead end)
- Social features (circles, mentors, stories are separate — not integrated)
- Life Engine gamification (capital, levels, zones — not connected to journey)
- Roadmap v1 (roadmap_templates table queried but not shown in journey flow)

## PART 6 — DUPLICATE CONCEPT AUDIT

| Concept A | Concept B | Verdict | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Roadmap** (v1 DB + `/roadmap` routes) | **Dream Journey** (`dream_journeys` + `/journey` routes) | **REMOVE Roadmap** | Same concept (template-based life plan). Migration 00009 header explicitly states Dream Journey "replaces" Roadmap. Roadmap v1 has older schema, no age filtering, no daily activities. Dream Journey is the successor. |
| **Roadmap v3** (`roadmap-v3-seed.ts` — rich content) | **Dream Templates** (`dream-templates.ts` — derived from v3) | **MERGE** | v3 is the content authoring format; `DreamTemplates` is the serialization layer. These should be unified under `dream_templates` as the canonical format. |
| **Daily Reflection** (`reflection-modal.tsx` + `daily_reflections` DB) | **RoadmapV3DailyReflections** (localStorage, `roadmap-v3-seed.ts`) | **REMOVE RoadmapV3 variant** | Two reflection UIs with different fields (3-field DB vs 5-field localStorage). The DB-backed journey reflection is the active path. |
| **Daily Reflection** (private, structured) | **Journal** (public, free-form, social) | **KEEP BOTH** | Different concepts. Reflection is daily check-in tied to journey. Journal is public diary with followers/reactions. But Journal needs a rebuild (currently localStorage-only). |
| **Life Engine** (`life-engine.ts` — capital, levels, zones) | **Dream Journey** (big wins, small wins, activities) | **REMOVE Life Engine** | Life Engine is client-side-only gamification running on localStorage. Dream Journey is DB-backed. The Life Engine's "growth zone" concept is a useful UX pattern but its implementation (capital, levels) duplicates what Dream Journey does with wins/activities. Migration 00009 explicitly states it "replaces Life Engine." |
| **Growth Zone** (comfort/fear/learning/growth) | **Growth Timeline** (`growth_timeline_events`) | **MERGE** | Zone is a client-side derivation from user actions. Timeline is a DB record of those actions. If timeline events are tracked, zone can be derived from them — no separate system needed. |
| **Age Journey groups** (8-12, 13-15, 16-18, 19-22, 23+) | **Life Engine stages** (sd/smp/sma/university/early-career/professional/mastery) | **KEEP Age Journey** | Age Journey groups are used for content filtering when creating journeys. Life Engine stages are pedagogical labels. Both serve different purposes, but Life Engine stages are the more universal concept (maps to Indonesian education levels). |
| **`ROADMAP_CATEGORIES`** (in `roadmap-seed.ts`) | **`CIRCLE_CATEGORIES`** (in `constants.ts`) | **UNIFY** | Different category sets for different features. Need a single canonical category taxonomy. |
| **`users.goals`** (TEXT[] column) | **`user_goals`** (dedicated table) | **REMOVE `users.goals`** | Duplicate storage. The `user_goals` table is the proper relational model. |
| **`getJourneyProgress()`** (derived function) | **`getBigWins()` + `getTodayActivities()` + `getTodayReflection()`** | **KEEP `getJourneyProgress`** | Already parallelized internally. It's a convenience function that composes 3 queries — useful for dashboards. |
| **Supabase queries in `queries.ts`** | **Server Actions in `app/actions/auth.ts`** | **PICK ONE** | Both implement auth (signUp, signIn, signOut). Keep Server Actions (next-gen pattern); remove `queries.ts` auth functions. |

## PART 8 — FOUNDER VISION ALIGNMENT

**Vision:** *"A positive life companion helping people discover dreams, pursue dreams, survive setbacks, grow holistically, build life stories, and become a better human. NOT a task manager, NOT a course platform, NOT a social media clone, NOT a roadmap repository."*

| Vision Area | Score | Gap |
|-------------|-------|-----|
| **Discover dreams** | **3/10** | No discovery mechanism. Users pick from 10 pre-defined templates. No quiz, no personality match, no wonder/curiosity layer. The `/discover` quiz exists but is deprecated (redirected to `/journey`). The `/onboarding` 7-step form exists but creates no journey. |
| **Pursue dreams** | **6/10** | Core flow works: pick template → view daily activities → complete → track wins. But it's essentially a checklist. No coaching, no guidance, no adaptive difficulty. Activities are generic (same set per category), not personalized. |
| **Survive setbacks** | **2/10** | `failure-modal.tsx` exists (encouragement when marking a big win as failed). That's it. No "pivot" flow, no alternative path suggestion, no community support, no mentor check-in. The `previous_dreams` table exists but is never used. |
| **Grow holistically** | **5/10** | 6 life dimensions (spiritual, physical, knowledge, social, character, dream_skill) is good. But dimensions are equally weighted every day — no personalization for user's weak areas. Life Engine's capital system was more nuanced but is orphaned. |
| **Build life stories** | **4/10** | `JourneyStory` component shows timeline of events + reflections. Good concept. But: no automated event capture (writing reflection doesn't create a timeline entry), no photo/rich media, no sharing, no "life story" export. |
| **NOT a task manager** | **5/10** | Despite the vision, the primary interaction IS a daily checklist. The app asks "what should I do today?" and shows 6 activities to check off. There's no narrative framing, no quest-like structure, no "why this matters" context per activity. |
| **NOT a course platform** | **9/10** | No structured courses, no lessons, no quizzes. The Roadmap v3 "masterclass" content exists in seed data but is never shown to users in the journey flow. |
| **NOT a social media clone** | **9/10** | Social features (circles, story comments, journal followers) exist minimally and are disconnected from the core flow. No news feed, no notifications, no profiles to follow. |
| **NOT a roadmap repository** | **4/10** | Despite the vision statement, the `/roadmap` route still exists and shows template data. The app was originally a "roadmap repository" and the legacy routes/tables/data are still there. The user experience on `/journey` (picking from a list of 10 templates) IS essentially a roadmap repository. |

**Overall vision alignment score: 5.2/10**

The app has moved away from its "roadmap repository" origins but hasn't fully arrived at the "life companion" vision. The core interaction is still a daily checklist. The dream discovery, setback survival, and life story building pillars are largely missing.

The naming inconsistency (brand="Beautifio", DB="dream_journeys", app route="/journey", seed data="roadmap-v3-seed") reflects the multi-generational architecture. Each layer was built in a different era.

### Migration 00009 header (the definitive statement):

> *"This migration creates the Dream Journey system. Replaces: Roadmap, Journal, Daily Wins, Life Engine, Growth Tracker, Reflections, Life Capital."*

This migration (header-written-as-comment) is the closest thing to an official product decision. It explicitly states that these 7 older systems are superseded. The problem: the older systems were never removed.
