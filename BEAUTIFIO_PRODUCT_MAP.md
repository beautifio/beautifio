## HOME

**Pillar:** Journey (daily execution)
**Status:** EXISTS — needs minimal adjustment

### Purpose

Home is the daily landing page. It answers the question "What do I do today?" without the user having to think. The user opens the app, sees their six daily activities, marks what they did, and closes the app. That is the core loop of Beautifio.

### Content

- Greeting (personalized, time-aware)
- 6 daily activity cards (one per dimension: career, health, mindset, skill, connection, rest)
- Quick-reflection prompt ("How did today feel?" — emoji picker)
- Today's Big Win name (context: "Working toward: [Big Win]")
- Optional: link to full reflection if user wants to write more

### User Outcome

The user completes their day in under 3 minutes. They feel a sense of quiet accomplishment — not because they did everything, but because they showed up. They did not have to decide what to do; the app decided for them.

### Why does this page exist?

Without Home, the user would have to navigate to a journey, find today's activities, and manually figure out what to do. Friction kills consistency. Home removes all friction from the daily loop.

### What would happen if we removed it?

The app loses its heartbeat. The daily practice dies. Users would log in less frequently because every visit requires a decision before an action. Retention collapses.

## JOURNEY

**Pillar:** Journey (structure + progress)
**Status:** EXISTS — needs Big Win/Small Win hierarchy enforced

### Purpose

Journey is the map. It shows the user where they are in their dream path. It answers "How far have I come?" and "What's next?" It contains the Big Win → Small Win → Activity hierarchy. This is the page for planning, not for doing.

### Content

- Current Dream name and description
- Big Win progression (stage-gated: beginner → intermediate → advanced → professional)
- Each Big Win shows its Small Wins (completed, in progress, locked)
- Celebration moment when all Small Wins in a Big Win are completed
- Pivot entry point (change dream / redirect)
- "Your Story" timeline link/embed (key moments, wins, reflections, pivots)

### User Outcome

The user sees their progress visualized. They feel momentum. They know what to work toward next. When they feel stuck, this page reminds them how far they've come. When they need to pivot, this page facilitates it without shame.

### Why does this page exist?

Without Journey, the user has no sense of direction or progress. The daily activities become disconnected tasks. The user cannot see the forest for the trees. Long-term engagement depends on the user feeling that their actions accumulate toward something meaningful.

### What would happen if we removed it?

The app reduces to a daily checklist with no context. Users stop caring about activities because they don't connect to anything larger. Completion rates drop. The app becomes a habit tracker (which the Constitution specifically prohibits).

## INSPIRATION

**Pillar:** Dream (discovery)
**Status:** DOES NOT EXIST — current Discover page is deprecated and must be rebuilt

### Purpose

Inspiration is the gateway. It is where the user discovers what dream to pursue. It answers "What could I do?" This is a guided exploration, not a directory. The user does not browse a list — they answer questions and receive a curated suggestion.

### Content

- Dream Discovery Quiz (interactive, 5-7 questions, no right answers)
  - "What do you enjoy doing?" (interests)
  - "What are you curious about?" (exploration)
  - "What kind of impact do you want to make?" (values)
  - "How much time can you commit?" (pacing)
  - "What's something you've always wanted to try?" (aspiration)
- Age-appropriate dream suggestions based on responses
- Dream preview (3-5 options with descriptions and "what you'll learn" summaries)
- "Try This Dream" button → creates journey and enters onboarding
- Undecided? Option to save quiz results and return later
- Browse alternative dreams (secondary, not primary — only after quiz is taken)

### User Outcome

The user goes from "I don't know what I want" to "I want to try [Dream]." They feel excitement, not overwhelm. They understand why the suggestion fits them. The decision feels personal, not algorithmic.

### Why does this page exist?

Without Inspiration, the user cannot start a journey. The discovery process is the most important moment in the user lifecycle — it determines whether they commit. A user who chooses their own dream from a pre-defined list has lower commitment than a user who was guided to a dream that feels tailored.

### What would happen if we removed it?

New users have no onboarding path. The only way to start a journey is to know what you want before you open the app. This excludes the primary audience (directionless young people). The app only serves people who already have a plan — which contradicts the core problem Beautifio solves.

## SAFE SPACE

**Pillar:** Support (internal — setbacks, failures, pivots)
**Status:** EXISTS (partial) — current reflection/journal features, needs consolidation

### Purpose

Safe Space is where the user goes when the journey gets hard. It is not a page you visit when things are fine — it is a page you visit when you are stuck, failed, pivoted, or feeling lost. It answers "What now?" and "What did I learn from this?"

### Content

- **Failure processing** (when a Big Win is marked as not completed):
  - "What did you learn?" (free text, no wrong answers)
  - Transferable skills surface ("You learned [skill] — this also applies to:")
  - Alternative futures (other dreams that use these skills)
- **Pivot flow:**
  - "You're changing direction. That's not starting over."
  - Visual: previous dream's completed skills carried forward
  - New dream selection (in-app, within Safe Space)
- **Setback journal:**
  - Private entries (what happened, how you feel, what you need)
  - Not visible on the Story timeline unless user chooses
  - Optional: "I need help" → connects to Support (real people)
- **Encouragement library:**
  - Past reflections review ("Remember when you wrote about [learned lesson]?")
  - Past wins review ("You completed [X] Small Wins. That's real progress.")

### User Outcome

When the user hits a setback, they do not abandon the app. The app meets them in that moment and reframes failure as data, not judgment. They feel seen, not pitied. They leave with a new direction, not a sense of defeat.

### Why does this page exist?

Without Safe Space, a setback equals churn. The user fails a Big Win or decides their dream isn't right — and has nowhere to process that inside the app. They close the tab and don't come back. Safe Space is the retention mechanism for the hardest moments in the user's journey.

### What would happen if we removed it?

Setback → Churn. Every pivot becomes an abandonment. The app feels good when everything is going well, but silent when the user needs it most. Long-term retention depends on this page because every long-term journey WILL include a setback.

## FAMILIA

**Pillar:** Familia (external resources)
**Status:** DOES NOT EXIST — must be built from scratch

### Purpose

Familia is the curated directory of real-world resources that support the user's journey. It answers "Where can I get real help?" for things the app cannot provide: scholarships, internships, mentorship programs, workshops, mental health support.

### Content

- Curated resource directory (categorized):
  - Scholarships & funding
  - Internships & apprenticeships
  - Mentorship programs
  - Skill workshops & bootcamps
  - Mental health resources
  - Communities & clubs
- Each resource:
  - Name, description, URL
  - Why it's relevant (curator note)
  - Which dreams it supports (tagged)
  - Trust rating (vetted by Beautifio — no user ratings)
- Save to journey (bookmark for later)
- Request a resource (user suggests — not guaranteed to be added)

### User Outcome

The user finds a real opportunity that moves their dream forward outside the app. They do not feel that Beautifio is a walled garden — it is a launchpad. They trust the recommendations because they know nothing is paid or promoted.

### Why does this page exist?

Without Familia, the app can guide a user toward a dream but cannot help them execute it in the real world. A dream of becoming a programmer needs a coding bootcamp or scholarship, not just 6 daily activities. Familia bridges the gap between intention and real-world action.

### What would happen if we removed it?

The app remains a motivational tool but never a practical one. Users who want to take real action have to find resources elsewhere. The app feels incomplete — it tells you what to do but not how to do it. The Familia pillar in the Constitution is left unfulfilled.

## PROFILE

**Pillar:** Meta (account + identity)
**Status:** EXISTS — needs cleanup (remove legacy data references)

### Purpose

Profile is where the user manages their account, preferences, and personal data. It answers "Who am I in this app?" and "What are my settings?" It is utility, not experience.

### Content

- Display name (anonymous or real)
- Age/date of birth (for content adaptation — stored, not displayed)
- Dream journey history (previous dreams, active dream)
- Preferences:
  - Notification settings (email vs in-app)
  - Privacy settings (data export, account deletion)
  - Theme / display preferences
- Account management:
  - Email, password, authentication methods
  - Delete account (with data export option)
- Activity summary (read-only):
  - Days active
  - Reflections written
  - Small wins completed
  - Big wins completed
  - Pivots made

### User Outcome

The user feels in control of their data and identity. They can leave the app at any time with their data. They can see their lifetime statistics as a motivational reference, not as a competitive scoreboard.

### Why does this page exist?

Without Profile, the user has no way to manage their account, change their preferences, or export their data. Privacy regulation (GDPR, COPPA) requires it. The user's lifetime stats are a secondary motivational tool that belongs in a utility context rather than the primary experience.

### What would happen if we removed it?

Legal non-compliance. Users cannot delete their data or manage their identity. The app feels like it owns the user rather than serving them. Trust erodes.
